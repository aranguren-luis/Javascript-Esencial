Burger for Dummies: 🍔

- A burger is, basically, a piece of some type of meat (or not) with some types of vegetables (or not), cheese, bacon and different types of sauces (or not), all of it placed in the middle of two pieces of bread.

  • Bread | 🍞
  • Lettuce | 🥬
  • Meat | 🥩
  • Onion | 🧅
  • Tomato | 🍅
  • Pickles | 🥒
  • Sauces | ❗
  • Bacon | 🥓
  • Cheese | 🧀
  1. Chop the tomatoes, onions and pickles
  2. Grill both the meat and bacon
  3. Once the meat is grilled, put the ingredientes on the bread
  4. Then put the sauces
  5. The Burger is done ✔️
